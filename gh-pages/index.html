About SMiPoly

“SMiPoly (Small Molecules into Polymers)” is rule-based virtual library generator for discovery of functional polymers. It is consist of two submodules, “monc.py” and “polg.py”. “monc.py” is a monomer classifier from a list of small molecules, and “polg.py” is a polymer repeating unit generator from the classified monomer list.
